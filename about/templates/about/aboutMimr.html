PURPOSE

The objective is simple: make ideas, make reality! Ideas are good, but are they enough? Ideas are the brains of a fully functional system, but without fueling the other moving parts, ideas are nothing but a useless series of electrochemical brain signals suppressed and held in captivity behind the bars of one's skull, unable to catch even a glimpse of the colors of the real world.

This would serve as a platform for the realization of the ideas generated from the daily life experiences and problems.
